The used datasets were not included in this submission, but they can be downloaded here:
- ml-100k: https://grouplens.org/datasets/movielens/100k/

copy the new data files to the datasets folder.
- polblogs: http://www-personal.umich.edu/~mejn/netdata/ -> "Political blogs"
